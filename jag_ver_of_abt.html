About Us

Introduction

Welcome to SkinWise, an AI-powered skin disease detection platform dedicated to making dermatological care more accessible and affordable. Our mission is to bridge the gap between technology and healthcare, providing rural and underserved communities with reliable skin health monitoring solutions.

Mission and Values

In many rural areas, access to dermatologists is limited, leaving common skin conditions untreated or improperly managed. SkinWise offers a simple yet effective way for users to assess their skin health through a question-based diagnosis system and AI-driven image analysis. Our goal is to empower individuals with knowledge and early detection, ensuring better health outcomes.

How it Works

  • Users can answer a set of predefined questions to describe their symptoms.
  • They can also upload photos for AI-based analysis.
  • Our system identifies possible skin conditions and provides recommendations.
  • If a serious issue is detected, users are advised to seek medical attention.
